Welcome to our New Online Community

Date: September 16,2025

Here you can find all the resources to connect with other Students, Alumni, Parents, Faculty and Staff.

Our USIU-Africa online community is a valuable lifelong resource platform for connecting with our continuously growing network of Students, Alumni, Parents, Faculty and Staff. The community presents unlimited opportunities in opening doors for stronger engagement, mentorship, collaboration and partnerships among each other and also with the University. In our 50+ years of history, we have always encouraged a spirit of community. This has enabled us to achieve significant milestones and become a premier institution, creating the legacy that we now experience and celebrate. Join our online community and have your opportunity to create your own personal & meaningful legacy for future generations..
